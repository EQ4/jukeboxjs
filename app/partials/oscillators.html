About Oscillators

Oscillators are part of the Web Audio API, and you never need to create one directly when using Jukebox.js. This section is a quick summary of how they work.

Oscillators have two states, on and off. When an oscillator is on, it produces sound. When an oscillator is off, it doesn't produce sound.

Additionally, Oscillators have two properties – frequency and type. The higher an ocsillator's frequency, the higher-pitched a noise it will make. An oscillator's type affects the shape of its wave, which is best explained by example.
