About Modulators

A modulator is a collection of zero or more oscillators and zero or more submodulators. Modulators are used to play single tones.

Modulators mostly exist to be composed into Synthesizers. However, they are still useful on their own if you need more individual control over sounds.

Modulators can implement any number of submodulators, which inherit the highest ancestor's frequency, and play and stop when it does.
